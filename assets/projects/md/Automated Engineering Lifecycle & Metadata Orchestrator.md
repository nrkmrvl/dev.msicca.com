## Project: Automated Engineering Lifecycle & Metadata Orchestrator

**A high-precision Master Document Register (MDR) and alphanumeric code generator.**

### 01. Vision & Impact

In large-scale engineering, a misplaced document or an incorrect naming convention can lead to catastrophic site errors or costly delays. We developed a proprietary **Document Control System** designed to automate the generation, registration, and tracking of technical assets. By centralizing the naming logic, we ensure 100% compliance with complex international engineering standards, reducing administrative overhead by approximately 40%.

### 02. The Engineering Challenge: "The Multi-Variant Naming Paradox"

The challenge was to translate a highly complex, multi-component alphanumeric syntax into a user-friendly interface that prevents human error. The system must handle mandatory identifiers and optional functional modifiers while maintaining a clean string structure.

* **The Solution:** A logic-driven **Semi-Automatic Generator** that uses dynamic concatenation and contextual filtering to build unique document identifiers in real-time.
