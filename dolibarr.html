FAQ

Common Questions

Is it compatible with my country's billing regulations?

Yes. Dolibarr supports electronic invoicing standards for multiple countries. We configure the system to comply with your local tax and billing requirements.

Can I use it from my mobile phone?

Absolutely. Dolibarr has a fully responsive web interface that adapts to any screen size. Access your CRM, invoices, and stock from your phone.

How long does implementation take?

Entrepreneur plans can be deployed in 1-2 weeks. SME implementations typically take 3-4 weeks. Enterprise solutions are scoped based on requirements.

Can I integrate it with other systems?

Yes. Dolibarr has a REST API for integrations. We can connect it with your existing tools, e-commerce platforms, payment processors, and more.
